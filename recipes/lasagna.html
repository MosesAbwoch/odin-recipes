Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it! Lasagna is a great Italian dish that is world-renowned. It is easily one of the most favored dishes here in America.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Cover with the foil and bake.
  5. Ler the lasagna rest before serving.
