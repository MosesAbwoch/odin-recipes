Chicken Alfredo

Description

This chicken Alfredo recipe with jar sauce is a quick and easy dinner for those hectic nights. For extra flavor, cook the chicken with lemon pepper and seasoning salt.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound uncooked fettuccine or linguine
  • 2 boneless chicken breast halves, cooked and cubed
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar Alfredo-style pasta sauce
  • 1 (10 ounce) package frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1 (4.5 ounce) can sliced mushrooms
  • 1/3 cup milk

Steps

  1. Fill a large pt with lightly slated water and bring to a rolling boil. Cook fettuccine at a boil until tender ter firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Drain well.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, place cubes cooked chicken, Alfredo sauce, frozen vegetables, mushrooms, and milk in a large saucepan over medium-low hear. Cook and stir until chicken is heated through and vegetables are tender.
  3. Serve warm Alfredo and chicken sauce over cooked noodles.
